Asian and Queer. A play that examines intersectionality.

What does it mean to be API and LGBT? Written by first time playwright Mr KL and directed by Mr C, DOMINIC explores what it means to be Asian in America, queer and Confucian, and how to stay hyphy while collecting hongbaos. Playing May 10-12, 2019 at the Bindlestorff Studio Theatre in San Francisco, CA. Tickets Out Now.
